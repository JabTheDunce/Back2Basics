Without a doubt the key to success is studying effectively and While there is no single "right way" to study, there are some general tips as well as countless techniques that can assist you in this endeavour for example the pomodoro technique.

One popular method for effective studying is the Pomodoro technique. This technique involves breaking down your study sessions into 25-minute intervals, separated by 5-minute breaks. Then after every fourth session, take a well deserved longer break of 15-30 minutes. This technique can help you stay focused and motivated while also providing regular opportunities for rest and recharging.

The importance of understanding the material actively, not just passively reading through it cannot be understated. Attempting to summarise the main ideas, make connections between concepts, and challenge yourself with questions about the material. Practising is also key to mastering any subject. Be sure to take advantage of practice tests, quizzes, and other resources to test your knowledge and identify areas where you may need additional help.
